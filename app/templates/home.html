    For Students

    Step 1

    Sign up yourself as a Student and then login your credentials.

    Step 2

    At student dashboard you will see your attendence where you can also filter it according to date and subject.

    Step 3

    Students Can view their respective Subjects Attendance.

    For Teachers

    Step 1

    Sign up yourself as a teacher and then login your credentials.

    Step 2

    Add your class and Subject. Upload the attendence excel file by selecting your class and subjects and then confirm

    Step 3

    At teacher dashboard you will see your attendences uploaded.You can also filter it according to date,subject and class.
